Controlling UPOS Accuracy

  • NCRF++: Sequence-labelling framework developed by Yang and Zhang (2018).
  • Bins: 2.5±0.3 from 60 to 80 and 1±0.3 from 80 onwards.
  • Parsers: For training used gold tags and a subset of accuracy bins (60, 70, 80, 86, 91, and 93). We used the whole range for gold trained parsers, but used the equivalent bin for the parsers trained with predicted tags.
